Let's Rewind

At Usagi Mountain Bike Park, our story begins with a deep-rooted love for the outdoors and a shared passion for mountain biking. The founders, who have always felt at home among the trails, mountains, and forests, envisioned a place where adventure and sustainability could come together. Their journey started with a simple yet powerful belief: nature and adventure should coexist in harmony, ensuring a legacy for future generations to enjoy.

The vision became reality when the owner inherited a beautiful expanse of land, nestled in the heart of nature. Inspired by the unspoiled beauty of the surroundings and driven by a commitment to preserving it, the idea of creating a sustainable mountain bike park took shape. This was not just about building a bike park; it was about creating a space where riders could connect with nature, push their limits, and experience the thrill of the trails, all while ensuring the park's environmental footprint was minimal.

At Usagi, sustainability is at the core of everything we do. From the ground up, we've worked hard to design a park that not only honors the land but enhances it. We use eco-friendly materials in trail construction, employ water management systems to protect natural resources, and ensure that our operations minimize waste. We also encourage our riders to embrace sustainability by promoting Leave No Trace principles and providing eco-conscious amenities. Sustainability isn’t just a buzzword for us—it’s essential to our mission of creating a place where both nature and adventure can thrive.

The name Usagi—the Japanese word for "rabbit"—was chosen to reflect the core values that drive us. In Japanese culture, the rabbit symbolizes advancement, adventure, longevity, and good fortune. These are the principles that guide Usagi Mountain Bike Park. Just like the rabbit's swift and agile movement through nature, we strive to constantly evolve, offering new adventures for riders of all levels. We aim for longevity, ensuring that the park remains a sustainable, cherished destination for generations to come. And, of course, we believe in good fortune—creating a positive impact on the environment, the community, and the cycling world.

Usagi Mountain Bike Park is more than just a place to ride; it's a destination where riders can experience the thrill of adventure in a setting that values and nurtures the very nature they enjoy. We are proud to offer a space where both the rider and the land can grow together.

Why Us?

A Sustainable Adventure

When you ride at Usagi, you’re not just enjoying thrilling trails—you’re also supporting a park that values sustainability. Our commitment to eco-friendly practices means that every trail, facility, and amenity has been carefully designed with the environment in mind. From our water conservation efforts to the materials we use in building our trails, we’re dedicated to ensuring the park thrives for generations to come. By choosing Usagi, you’re riding with purpose, knowing your adventure helps protect the very landscape you love.

World-Class Trails for All Levels

Whether you’re a seasoned mountain biker or just starting out, Usagi has something for everyone. Our park features a wide variety of trails, from scenic cross-country routes to adrenaline-pumping downhill runs. Carefully crafted with riders in mind, our trails offer a range of difficulty levels, so you can challenge yourself, improve your skills, and explore new paths at your own pace. Every ride is an adventure, and every turn brings something new to discover.

A Place for Community and Connection

Usagi isn’t just a park—it’s a community. Our visitors come from all walks of life, united by their love for biking and the outdoors. We foster an inclusive, welcoming environment where riders can meet new people, share tips, and support one another. Whether you’re here for a solo adventure or to ride with friends, Usagi offers a space to connect, learn, and grow as part of a passionate biking family.

Stunning Natural Beauty

Riding at Usagi means immersing yourself in some of the most breathtaking landscapes around. Surrounded by lush forests, rugged mountains, and wide-open skies, our park offers a peaceful yet exhilarating backdrop for every ride. You'll find yourself reconnecting with nature, escaping the hustle and bustle of everyday life, and discovering the beauty of the great outdoors with every pedal stroke.

Commitment to Longevity and Adventure

Our name, Usagi, means “rabbit” in Japanese and symbolizes advancement, adventure, longevity, and good fortune. These values guide everything we do. We are constantly evolving to offer new and exciting challenges while maintaining a focus on sustainability and long-term growth. At Usagi, we believe every adventure should leave a lasting impact—on you, on the community, and on the environment.

A Safe and Well-Maintained Park

Your safety is our priority. Our park is designed with rider safety in mind, featuring well-marked trails, ample signage, and expert maintenance to ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience. Whether you’re racing down a technical trail or cruising through scenic landscapes, you can rest assured that Usagi is a safe and well-maintained place to ride.
