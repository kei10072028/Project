Policies

Financing Policies

At Usagi Mountain Bike Park, we understand that purchasing a mountain bike and equipment can be a significant investment. That's why we offer flexible financing options to make it easier for you to get the gear you need to enjoy the adventure. Our goal is to help you get on the trails without the burden of upfront costs.

Our Financing Options Include:

0% Interest Financing: For qualified buyers, we offer 6, 12, or 18-month interest-free payment plans. This way, you can split your payments and enjoy your bike today without the worry of high interest rates.

Flexible Payment Terms: Choose the payment schedule that works best for you—weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly.

Easy Approval Process: Our quick and simple application process can be done in-store or online, and you’ll get an instant approval decision.

No Hidden Fees: Our financing options are transparent, so you won’t encounter any hidden fees. The price you see is the price you pay, divided into manageable payments.

How to Apply:

Visit our shop or contact us online to apply for financing.

Provide the required information, such as proof of income, and choose your preferred payment plan.

Once approved, you’ll be able to take your bike and gear home the same day or get your order delivered to your home.

If you have any questions about financing or need help with the application process, feel free to reach out to our customer service team.

Delivery Policies

We’re committed to getting your new gear to you as quickly and efficiently as possible so you can start your adventure with Usagi Mountain Bike Park. Our delivery policies are designed to provide a seamless experience for our customers.

Delivery Options:

Standard Delivery: Enjoy delivery to your door within 5-7 business days after your order is processed. This service is available for all bikes, accessories, and apparel.

Express Delivery: Need it sooner? Our express delivery option ensures your order arrives within 2-3 business days, so you can hit the trails as quickly as possible.

Local Pickup: If you’re in the area, you can opt for local pickup and collect your order directly from our bike park. Just come by during our regular business hours, and we’ll have it ready for you.

Shipping Costs:

We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $100.

For orders under $100, a shipping fee of $10 will apply for standard delivery.

Express shipping fees vary depending on the size and weight of your order. You’ll be able to see the exact shipping cost at checkout.

Tracking Your Order: Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package's progress and estimated delivery time.

International Shipping: We currently offer international shipping to select countries. Please contact our customer service team for more details on international orders.

Return Policies

We want you to love your gear from Usagi Mountain Bike Park. However, if for any reason you need to return an item, our return policy is simple and hassle-free.

Return Eligibility:

30-Day Return Window: You have 30 days from the date of purchase to return your items. Whether you purchased in-store or online, we want you to be fully satisfied with your purchase.

Condition of Returns: To be eligible for a return, the product must be in new, unused condition with all original packaging and tags intact. Bikes must not have been ridden.

Exclusions: Custom bikes, used bikes, or opened apparel cannot be returned unless they are defective.

How to Return:

In-Store Returns: Visit our bike park during regular business hours, and bring your receipt or order confirmation. Our staff will assist you with your return.

Online Returns: To return an item purchased online, contact us to initiate a return. Once approved, we’ll provide you with a return shipping label. You are responsible for the cost of return shipping unless the item is defective or damaged.

Refund Process:

Once your return is received and processed, we will issue a refund to the original payment method. Please allow 7-10 business days for the refund to be processed and reflected in your account.

If you made your purchase using financing, your refund will be processed through your financing provider, and the payment balance will be adjusted accordingly.

Damaged or Defective Items: If you receive a damaged or defective product, please contact us immediately at headoffice@usagibikepark.com or call +1 (234) 567-890. We will arrange a free return or replacement and cover the shipping costs for the return.
