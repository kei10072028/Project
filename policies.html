Financing Policies

At Usagi Mountain Bike Park, we understand that purchasing a mountain bike and equipment can be a significant investment. That's why we offer flexible financing options to make it easier for you to get the gear you need to enjoy the adventure. Our goal is to help you get on the trails without the burden of upfront costs.

Our Financing Options Include:

0% Interest Financing: For qualified buyers, we offer 6, 12, or 18-month interest-free payment plans. This way, you can split your payments and enjoy your bike today without the worry of high interest rates.

Flexible Payment Terms: Choose the payment schedule that works best for you—weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly. Easy Approval Process: Our quick and simple application process can be done in-store or online, and you’ll get an instant approval decision.

No Hidden Fees: Our financing options are transparent, so you won’t encounter any hidden fees. The price you see is the price you pay, divided into manageable payments.

How to Apply:

Visit our shop or contact us online to apply for financing.

Provide the required information, such as proof of income, and choose your preferred payment plan.

Once approved, you’ll be able to take your bike and gear home the same day or get your order delivered to your home.

If you have any questions about financing or need help with the application process, feel free to reach out to our customer service team.

Delivery Policies

We’re committed to getting your new gear to you as quickly and efficiently as possible so you can start your adventure with Usagi Mountain Bike Park. Our delivery policies are designed to provide a seamless experience for our customers. Delivery Options: Standard Delivery: Enjoy delivery to your door within 5-7 business days after your order is processed. This service is available for all bikes, accessories, and apparel. Express Delivery: Need it sooner? Our express delivery option ensures your order arrives within 2-3 business days, so you can hit the trails as quickly as possible. Local Pickup: If you’re in the area, you can opt for local pickup and collect your order directly from our bike park. Just come by during our regular business hours, and we’ll have it ready for you. Shipping Costs: We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $100. For orders under $100, a shipping fee of $10 will apply for standard delivery. Express shipping fees vary depending on the size and weight of your order. You’ll be able to see the exact shipping cost at checkout. Tracking Your Order: Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package's progress and estimated delivery time. International Shipping: We currently offer international shipping to select countries. Please contact our customer service team for more details on international orders.

Return Policies

We want you to love your gear from Usagi Mountain Bike Park. However, if for any reason you need to return an item, our return policy is simple and hassle-free. Return Eligibility: 30-Day Return Window: You have 30 days from the date of purchase to return your items. Whether you purchased in-store or online, we want you to be fully satisfied with your purchase. Condition of Returns: To be eligible for a return, the product must be in new, unused condition with all original packaging and tags intact. Bikes must not have been ridden. Exclusions: Custom bikes, used bikes, or opened apparel cannot be returned unless they are defective. How to Return: In-Store Returns: Visit our bike park during regular business hours, and bring your receipt or order confirmation. Our staff will assist you with your return. Online Returns: To return an item purchased online, contact us to initiate a return. Once approved, we’ll provide you with a return shipping label. You are responsible for the cost of return shipping unless the item is defective or damaged. Refund Process: Once your return is received and processed, we will issue a refund to the original payment method. Please allow 7-10 business days for the refund to be processed and reflected in your account. If you made your purchase using financing, your refund will be processed through your financing provider, and the payment balance will be adjusted accordingly. Damaged or Defective Items: If you receive a damaged or defective product, please contact us immediately at [Insert email address] or call [Insert phone number]. We will arrange a free return or replacement and cover the shipping costs for the return.

Why Us?

A Sustainable Adventure

When you ride at Usagi, you’re not just enjoying thrilling trails—you’re also supporting a park that values sustainability. Our commitment to eco-friendly practices means that every trail, facility, and amenity has been carefully designed with the environment in mind. From our water conservation efforts to the materials we use in building our trails, we’re dedicated to ensuring the park thrives for generations to come. By choosing Usagi, you’re riding with purpose, knowing your adventure helps protect the very landscape you love.

World-Class Trails for All Levels

Whether you’re a seasoned mountain biker or just starting out, Usagi has something for everyone. Our park features a wide variety of trails, from scenic cross-country routes to adrenaline-pumping downhill runs. Carefully crafted with riders in mind, our trails offer a range of difficulty levels, so you can challenge yourself, improve your skills, and explore new paths at your own pace. Every ride is an adventure, and every turn brings something new to discover.

A Place for Community and Connection

Usagi isn’t just a park—it’s a community. Our visitors come from all walks of life, united by their love for biking and the outdoors. We foster an inclusive, welcoming environment where riders can meet new people, share tips, and support one another. Whether you’re here for a solo adventure or to ride with friends, Usagi offers a space to connect, learn, and grow as part of a passionate biking family.

Stunning Natural Beauty

Riding at Usagi means immersing yourself in some of the most breathtaking landscapes around. Surrounded by lush forests, rugged mountains, and wide-open skies, our park offers a peaceful yet exhilarating backdrop for every ride. You'll find yourself reconnecting with nature, escaping the hustle and bustle of everyday life, and discovering the beauty of the great outdoors with every pedal stroke.

Commitment to Longevity and Adventure

Our name, Usagi, means “rabbit” in Japanese and symbolizes advancement, adventure, longevity, and good fortune. These values guide everything we do. We are constantly evolving to offer new and exciting challenges while maintaining a focus on sustainability and long-term growth. At Usagi, we believe every adventure should leave a lasting impact—on you, on the community, and on the environment.

A Safe and Well-Maintained Park

Your safety is our priority. Our park is designed with rider safety in mind, featuring well-marked trails, ample signage, and expert maintenance to ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience. Whether you’re racing down a technical trail or cruising through scenic landscapes, you can rest assured that Usagi is a safe and well-maintained place to ride.
